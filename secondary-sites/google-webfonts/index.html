Hopefully this document will be useful in selecting fonts for your projects. A collection of popular Google fonts is arrayed below (in no particular order), briefly demonstrating the basic feel of each.


For convenience, the related html <head> links and css selectors are listed in the code section below. Simply copying and pasting the relevant fields into your project should immediately enable use. Thanks for visiting, and have a great day!
